Three Nudges
A moment between impulse and action

When you reach for a distracting app, PauseMate interrupts the habit loop with a gentle, mindful pause. You pick the type.

Breathe

A guided breathing exercise with visual and haptic feedback. Complete a cycle and the impulse often passes on its own.

Level 1

Reflect

A short question that asks why you're reaching for this app right now. Build self-awareness without self-judgement.

Level 2

Wait

A simple timed delay. 15 to 45 seconds of stillness. Enough time for the autopilot to release and your intention to surface.

How It Works
Escalating friction

Nudges get progressively deeper with each re-open within an hour. The more you reach, the more space you're given to notice.

1

You reach for an app

Open Instagram, TikTok, or any app you've chosen to pause on. PauseMate catches the moment before autopilot kicks in.

2

A gentle nudge appears

First time: a breathing exercise. Second time within an hour: a reflection prompt. Third: a timed delay. Each creates more space.

3

You decide what's next

Complete the nudge and the app unlocks for 2 minutes. You're always in control. The choice was yours all along.

Features
Mindful by design

Mindfulness Streaks

Set recurring focus blocks for work hours, study time, or wind-down. PauseMate activates automatically.

When you bypass a nudge, capture your reason. Over time, patterns reveal which habits need the most attention.
