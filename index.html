The Science
This isn't about willpower.
It's about how your brain works.

Decades of behavioural research show that a brief pause at the right moment changes everything. PauseMate is built on that research.

47%

fewer app opens with escalating friction

An interstitial pause screen before app opening reduced visits by 13% at low friction, 27% at medium, and 47% at high — mirroring PauseMate's escalating nudge design.

Kim, Park, Lee, Ko & Lee, 2019. ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems. KAIST.

followed through with a specific pause plan

People who set a concrete "when X happens, I will do Y" plan completed their goal 71% of the time — compared to 32% who simply intended to do it.

Gollwitzer & Brandstätter, 1997. Journal of Personality and Social Psychology.

How It Works
Three nudges. Escalating friction.

When you reach for a distracting app, PauseMate interrupts the habit loop. Nudges get deeper with each re-open within an hour.

1

Breathe

A guided breathing exercise with visual and haptic feedback. Complete one cycle — the impulse often passes on its own.

A focused breathing exercise reduces emotional reactivity — Arch & Craske, 2006
2

Reflect

A short question: why are you reaching for this app right now? Builds self-awareness without self-judgement.

Reappraisal outperforms suppression for regulating emotions — Gross, 2002
3

Wait

A timed delay. 15 to 45 seconds of stillness. Enough time for the autopilot to release and your intention to surface.

Escalating friction reduced app visits by up to 47% — Kim et al., CHI 2019
Complete any nudge and the app unlocks. You're always in control.
Features
Mindful by design

Every feature respects your autonomy while building better habits.

Focus Sessions

When you want zero temptation, choose to fully block apps for 25, 50, or 90 minutes. Your boundary, on your terms.

Weekly Attention Audit

A 7-day summary of app opens, pauses completed, and bypass patterns. Insights, not guilt trips.

Mindfulness Streaks

Track consecutive days of completing nudges. Gentle motivation that rewards presence, not punishment.

Intent Capture

When you bypass a nudge, capture your reason. Over time, patterns reveal which habits need the most attention.
