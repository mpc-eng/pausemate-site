2. Description of Service

PauseMate is a mindfulness-based screen time awareness app for iOS. It creates gentle pauses before you open apps you find distracting, using Apple's FamilyControls and ManagedSettings frameworks. The App is designed to promote intentional device usage through breathing exercises, reflection prompts, and timed delays.

PauseMate does not block apps. It creates a brief moment of awareness before apps open. You are always in control and can choose to proceed at any time.
